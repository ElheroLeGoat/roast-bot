2018-08-07:

  • Roast-Bot v1.7.0 is live! The new features of this release are:
  • r!level, Roast-Bot now has a XP-System, the XP-System only increases your XP when you use a Roast-Bot command! No need to worry about it interfering with other bots XP-Systems. There are 10 levels. Every time you use a Roast-Bot command your XP increases by 1. The XP required for each is documented below:

    • Level 1: 0-9XP
    Level 2: 10XP
    Level 3: 15XP
    Level 4: 25XP
    Level 5: 50XP
    Level 6: 100XP
    Level 7: 200XP
    Level 8: 500XP
    Level 9: 1,000XP
    Level 10: 10,000XP

  • Please be aware that r!level is in a Beta stage(Some features may change or be reworked). The XP system was made with Enmap, https://www.npmjs.com/package/enmap enjoy!
For more information send me a DM or ask in #roast-bot-help.
